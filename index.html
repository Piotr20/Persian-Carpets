Explore the creative process behind traditional Persian carpet making.

ARTIST

Renowned artists often leave their signature on the top of their work as a sign of recognition. Carpets made by famous artists have reached prices up to 120000 DKK.

MATERIAL

Our carpets are made from cotton, wool or silk. Wool is most commonly used as it is both soft and durable. Silk is more expensive as it is harder to obtain, but has the advantage of being extremely fine.

TECHNIQUE

An age old traditional art form and way of textile production unique to Iran. Where two sets of yarn are interlaced at right angles to form fabric.

DYES

All of our carpets are colored using organic vegetable dyes rather than chemical dyes. Giving them a beautiful look and a natural feel.

SYMBOLISM

Each craftsman has a narrative in mind when they select the colors and motifs for their original rug. In order to truly appreciate these beautiful works and how all of their hues work together, you must first learn about the meaning of each individual color.
